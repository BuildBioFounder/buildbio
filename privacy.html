1. Information We Collect

BuildBio collects information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the Platform:

Account InfoEmail, username, password (hashed)Authentication, account management

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the BuildBio platform
  • To display public vehicle profiles to QR code scanners
  • To calculate and distribute affiliate revenue share
  • To send scan milestone notifications and recall alerts
  • To generate aggregate, anonymized data insights (B2B data product — no personally identifiable information sold)

3. Information Sharing

BuildBio does not sell personal information. We share data only in these circumstances:

  • Public profile data: Vehicle profiles are public by default. Anyone who scans a QR code or visits a profile URL sees the vehicle information, modifications, and owner display name.
  • Aggregate B2B data: Scan patterns, modification trends, and market insights — always anonymized, never individual-level.
  • Service providers: Supabase (hosting/auth), Stripe (payments), MailerSend (email).
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. QR Scan Privacy

When someone scans a BuildBio QR code, we record: timestamp, approximate city/state (not precise GPS), and device type. This data is visible only to the vehicle owner. Scanners are not required to create an account or identify themselves.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the life of the account. Upon account deletion request, personal data is removed within 30 days. Anonymized aggregate data may be retained indefinitely.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Portability: Export your vehicle and modification data
  • Opt-out: Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact founder@buildbio.app.

7. Cookies and Tracking

BuildBio uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising trackers at this time.

8. Children's Privacy

BuildBio is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or platform notification.

10. Contact

Privacy questions: founder@buildbio.app
