Everything you need to know about BuildBio, vehicle profiles, QR codes, and pricing.

What is BuildBio?
+
BuildBio is the social platform for modified vehicles. Every build gets its own public profile page with a dynamic QR code. Scan it to see the full mod list, specs, photos, and purchase links -- instantly, on any phone, no app required.
Is BuildBio really free?
+
Yes. The Free tier is free forever -- no credit card required. It includes one vehicle profile, a dynamic QR code, full mod listings, and a public profile page. Paid tiers unlock additional features like revenue share, analytics, and ad-free browsing.
What is founding member pricing?
+
Founding members get discounted rates locked in for an extended period. Mid tier: $6.99/mo (locked for 1 year, standard $9.99). Premium tier: $19.99/mo (locked for 3 years, standard $24.99). Founding member spots are limited.
How does the QR code work?
+
Every vehicle profile gets a unique, dynamic QR code. Unlike static codes, it never needs reprinting -- update your mods anytime and the code always points to your live profile. Download it for your digital wallet, share it on social media, or order a weatherproof vinyl sticker for your vehicle.
How does revenue share work?
+
Premium members earn a percentage (5-30%) of every affiliate sale generated through their profile. When someone scans a QR code, views the build, and purchases a part through an affiliate link, the profile owner earns a revenue share. Payouts are monthly on the 15th with a $25 minimum threshold.
What types of vehicles can I list?
+
Any modified vehicle. Trucks, Jeeps, off-road rigs, sports cars, imports, classics, motorcycles -- if it has aftermarket modifications, it belongs on BuildBio. All builds welcome.
