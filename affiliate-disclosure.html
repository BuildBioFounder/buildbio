Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how BuildBio earns revenue and shares it with you.

How BuildBio Earns Revenue

BuildBio participates in affiliate programs with automotive parts retailers and manufacturers. When a visitor clicks an affiliate link on a vehicle profile and makes a purchase, BuildBio may earn a commission from the retailer at no additional cost to the buyer.

Revenue Share With Vehicle Owners

Eligible Premium members earn a portion of affiliate commissions generated through their vehicle profiles. This is BuildBio's core value proposition: your build pays you back.

Key Terms

  • Attribution window: 60 days from click to purchase

What This Means for You

As a visitor: Affiliate links do not change the price you pay. You will always see the same price whether you click through BuildBio or go directly to the retailer's website.

As a vehicle owner: Parts listed on your profile may include affiliate links. When a visitor purchases through your profile, you earn a percentage of the commission based on your tier.

Honest Recommendations

BuildBio does not alter modification listings based on affiliate partnerships. Owners list the parts they actually run. The platform does not recommend, rank, or promote products based on affiliate commission rates.

Eligibility

Participation is available exclusively to active Premium tier members in good standing. Free and Mid tier members are ineligible. The Affiliate Program will become available no earlier than six months after the Platform's initial launch date.

How It Works

When a visitor to your BuildBio vehicle profile clicks an affiliate Buy Now link and completes a qualifying purchase within the attribution window, BuildBio earns a commission from the affiliate partner or retains a margin on BuildShop catalog sales. BuildBio then shares a portion of that commission with you based on your performance tier.

Affiliate links are automatically embedded in your vehicle profile's modification listings. You do not create or manage affiliate links directly.

Commission Structure

Affiliate Purchases: BuildBio earns approximately 8-12% from affiliate partners. You earn a percentage of BuildBio's earned commission based on your performance tier.

BuildShop Catalog: BuildBio earns approximately 30-50% gross margin. Your percentage rate is lower than affiliate rates, but absolute dollar earnings per transaction are equal to or greater due to BuildBio's higher margin.

Attribution and Exclusions

  • 60-day window: You earn commission only on qualifying purchases completed within 60 days of a visitor clicking your affiliate link or adding a product to their BuildShop cart from your profile
  • Dream Build: Attributable to you if completed within 60 days of the wishlist add event using a last-touch attribution model
  • Excluded: Purchases by free tier visitors, Dream Build purchases from any profile, AI-powered recommendation purchases, and purchases after the 60-day window

Payment Terms

  • Payments processed through Stripe Connect
  • Monthly schedule, 15th of each month
  • Minimum threshold: $25.00
  • Earnings cleared after affiliate partner's 30-day return/refund window
  • 1099 tax forms issued as required by law

Prohibited Conduct

The following result in immediate termination and forfeiture of all pending earnings:

  • Self-purchasing through your own affiliate links
  • Purchasing through related parties' links to earn commissions
  • Artificially inflating click or conversion metrics
  • Misrepresenting BuildBio or affiliated brands
  • Using spam, deceptive advertising, or misleading content
  • Creating fake reviews or ratings

FTC Disclosure Requirements

When promoting your BuildBio profile or affiliated products on any external platform (social media, YouTube, website, etc.), you are required to disclose your material connection to BuildBio and any financial benefit you receive, in compliance with FTC Endorsement Guidelines.

Not Employment

This program does not create an employment, agency, franchise, joint venture, or partnership relationship between BuildBio and you. You are an independent participant in the Program.
