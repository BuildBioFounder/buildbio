Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how BuildBio earns revenue and shares it with you.

How BuildBio Earns Revenue

BuildBio participates in affiliate programs with automotive parts retailers and manufacturers. When a visitor clicks an affiliate link on a vehicle profile and makes a purchase, BuildBio may earn a commission from the retailer at no additional cost to the buyer.

Revenue Share With Vehicle Owners

Eligible Premium members earn a portion of affiliate commissions generated through their vehicle profiles. This is BuildBio's core value proposition: your build pays you back.

Key Terms

  • Attribution window: 60 days from click to purchase
  • Minimum payout: $25.00
  • Payment schedule: Monthly, 15th of each month
  • Payment method: Stripe Connect (direct deposit)

What This Means for You

As a visitor: Affiliate links do not change the price you pay. You will always see the same price whether you click through BuildBio or go directly to the retailer's website.

As a vehicle owner: Parts listed on your profile may include affiliate links. When a visitor purchases through your profile, you earn a percentage of the commission based on your tier.

Honest Recommendations

BuildBio does not alter modification listings based on affiliate partnerships. Owners list the parts they actually run. The platform does not recommend, rank, or promote products based on affiliate commission rates.

Affiliate Program Agreement

This Affiliate Program Agreement ("Agreement") governs participation in the BuildBio Affiliate Program ("Program") by Premium tier members ("Affiliate" or "you"). This Agreement is incorporated into and subject to the BuildBio Terms of Service.

1. Program Eligibility

Participation in the Program is available exclusively to active Premium tier members in good standing. Free and Mid tier members are ineligible. Premium membership must remain active and payments current for affiliate earnings to accumulate and be paid. Suspension or termination of your Premium membership for any reason results in immediate suspension of affiliate earnings accrual.

2. How the Program Works

When a visitor to your BuildBio vehicle profile clicks an affiliate Buy Now link and completes a qualifying purchase within the attribution window, BuildBio earns a commission from the third-party affiliate partner or retains a margin on BuildShop catalog sales. BuildBio then shares a portion of that commission with you as described in Section 3.

Affiliate links are automatically embedded in your vehicle profile's modification listings. You do not create or manage affiliate links directly.

Note on Program Availability. The Affiliate Program is not available at initial Platform launch. The Program will become available to eligible Premium members no earlier than six months after the Platform's initial launch date. You will be notified by email when the Program activates.

3. Commission Rates

3.1 Affiliate Purchases. For purchases made through third-party affiliate partners via your profile, BuildBio earns a commission of approximately 8–12% of the eligible sale price. You earn a percentage of BuildBio's earned commission based on your performance tier as set forth in the Revenue Share Rate Schedule maintained on the Platform, which may be updated with 30 days' notice.

3.2 BuildShop Catalog Purchases. For purchases made through the BuildShop catalog, BuildBio earns a gross margin of approximately 30–50% of the eligible sale price. You earn a percentage of the retail sale price based on your performance tier as set forth in the BuildShop Rate Schedule. While the percentage rate is lower than affiliate rates, the absolute dollar earnings per transaction are equal to or greater than equivalent affiliate purchases due to BuildBio's higher margin.

3.3 Performance Tiers. Your commission rate is determined monthly based on total referred eligible sales during that calendar month. Tier status resets on the first day of each month.

3.4 Commissions are calculated on pre-tax, pre-shipping eligible sale prices.

4. Attribution Windows and Exclusions

4.1 Attribution is tracked via BuildBio's proprietary tracking technology. BuildBio's attribution determinations are final.

4.2 60-Day Window. You earn commission only on qualifying purchases completed within 60 days of a visitor clicking your affiliate link or adding a product to their BuildShop cart from your profile. Purchases made after 60 days generate revenue retained entirely by BuildBio.

4.3 Dream Build. Purchases made by visitors who added products to their Dream Build wishlist from your profile are attributable to you if completed within 60 days of the wishlist add event using a last-touch attribution model.

4.4 Excluded Purchases. No commission is earned on: purchases made by free tier profile visitors; Dream Build purchases completed from any profile; AI-powered recommendation purchases; and purchases completed after the applicable 60-day attribution window.

5. Payment

5.1 Earnings are paid through Stripe Connect. You must complete Stripe Connect onboarding to receive payments. BuildBio is not responsible for delays caused by incomplete onboarding.

5.2 Payments are processed on a monthly schedule on the 15th of each month for earnings that have cleared the affiliate partner's return/refund window (typically 30 days after purchase).

5.3 Earnings below $25.00 will accumulate until the threshold is reached before a payment is processed.

5.4 BuildBio will issue 1099 tax forms as required by applicable law. You are responsible for all taxes on your affiliate earnings.

6. Prohibited Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate termination of Program participation and forfeiture of all pending earnings:

  • Self-purchasing through your own affiliate links
  • Purchasing through the affiliate links of related parties for the purpose of earning commissions
  • Artificially inflating click or conversion metrics
  • Misrepresenting BuildBio, its products, or affiliated brands in any communications
  • Using spam, deceptive advertising, or misleading content to drive traffic
  • Creating fake reviews or ratings to manipulate product listings

7. FTC Disclosure Requirements

When promoting your BuildBio profile or its affiliated products on any external platform (social media, YouTube, website, etc.), you are required to disclose your material connection to BuildBio and any financial benefit you receive from purchases made through your profile, in compliance with FTC Endorsement Guidelines. BuildBio is not responsible for your compliance with FTC requirements.

8. Termination

BuildBio may terminate your Program participation at any time for violation of this Agreement or the Terms of Service. You may discontinue Program participation at any time by downgrading your membership. Pending earned commissions for completed, non-disputed transactions will be paid upon termination subject to the payment schedule and minimum threshold.

9. Modification

BuildBio may modify commission rates, attribution rules, or Program terms with 30 days' notice. Continued participation after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the modifications.

10. No Employment or Partnership

This Agreement does not create an employment, agency, franchise, joint venture, or partnership relationship between BuildBio and you. You are an independent participant in the Program.
