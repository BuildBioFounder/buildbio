About BuildBio

Built by car people
for car people

Every modified vehicle has a story. BuildBio gives it a permanent home.

The mission

BuildBio exists to give every modified vehicle its own digital identity. One QR code, one profile page, every mod documented and discoverable. No more forum digs. No more comment-section hunting. Scan it and know.

We are building the definitive platform for the aftermarket vehicle community -- a place where owners document their builds, viewers discover parts they want, and the entire ecosystem grows together.

What drives us

Three principles guide every product decision at BuildBio.

01
Owner-first
Every feature starts with one question: does this make life better for the person who built the vehicle? If yes, ship it. If not, cut it.
02
Accuracy matters
Verified builds, 90-day accuracy confirmations, real part numbers. The car community deserves data it can trust.
03
Builders get paid
Your build generates affiliate interest every time someone scans it. Premium members earn revenue share on every sale. Your build pays you back.
