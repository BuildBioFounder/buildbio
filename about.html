About BuildBio

Built by car people.For car people.

BuildBio started with a simple frustration: you see an incredible build and there's no easy way to know what's on it. We fixed that.

The mission

Give every build a permanent home

BuildBio exists to give every modified vehicle its own digital identity. One QR code, one profile page, every mod documented and discoverable. No more forum digs. No more comment-section hunting. No more unanswered DMs. Scan it and know.

The aftermarket vehicle community generates billions in parts sales every year, but the builds themselves — the actual documentation of what people install, how it performs, and why they chose it — lives scattered across Instagram captions, forum threads, and group chats that disappear. BuildBio makes that information permanent, searchable, and useful.

Every mod. Every build.
The trail doesn't ask what you paid.

What drives us

Three principles. Zero exceptions.

Every feature, every design decision, and every line of code at BuildBio filters through these.

01
Owner-first
Every feature starts with one question: does this make life better for the person who built the vehicle? If yes, ship it. If not, cut it. The owner is the customer, the content creator, and the reason this platform exists.
02
Accuracy matters
Verified builds, 90-day accuracy confirmations, real part numbers. The car community has been burned by bad data for too long — forum posts from 2014, dead links, wrong fitment. BuildBio data is current or it gets flagged.
03
Builders get paid
Your build generates affiliate interest every time someone scans it. Premium members earn revenue share on every sale their profile drives. Your build isn't just documented — it pays you back.

The founder

Meet Blakeley

Blakeley King
Founder

BuildBio was born at a Cars and Coffee. Blakeley saw a 4Runner with a setup he'd never seen before — clean lift, perfect wheel fitment, light bar he couldn't identify. The owner wasn't around. He DM'd them on Instagram. No response. He dug through forums. Dead ends. Three hours later, still no straight answer.

That's the moment BuildBio clicked. Every modified vehicle should have its own page — scannable, permanent, and complete. One QR code that tells the whole story.

Blakeley's background isn't Silicon Valley. Six years in automotive sales — he knows how people talk about cars, what matters at the point of sale, and what information actually closes the gap between curiosity and purchase. Before that, fine dining and sales. The thread through all of it: understanding what people want before they can articulate it.

BuildBio is bootstrapped, solo-founded, and built in Westlake Village, California. No venture capital. No board of directors. Just a founder who got tired of unanswered DMs and decided to build the fix himself.
