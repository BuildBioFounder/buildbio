How the Floor Works
The Ambassador/Influencer designation sets a permanent minimum commission rate. If your earned tier (based on monthly sales) is higher than your floor, you keep the higher rate. If your earned tier dips below, the floor catches you. You never earn less than your designated minimum -- even in slow months.
