Why BuildBio?

Tired of texting or DM'ing your mod list to strangers just so they can find out what's on your build -- and giving out your personal info in the process?

We've all been there. Hunting through comment sections, sliding into DMs, scrolling five platforms deep trying to find one part name -- only to hit a dead end.

BuildBio was built by an enthusiast, for enthusiasts.

List every mod on your vehicle in one clean, scannable profile. Share it at Cars and Coffee, car meets, trail runs, or anywhere online with a unique QR code.

One snap of the camera and anyone has instant access to your full build -- every part, every brand, easy to read, easy to research, easy to buy. No more dead ends. No more DMs. Just your build, documented.
